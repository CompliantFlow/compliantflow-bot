Built in Europe for Europe, EU Compliant by Design

Unlike generic chatbot providers, CompliantFlow is designed from day one to meet the strictest European standards. This saves you time and money, and protects you against unexpected compliance issues.

  • AI chatbots designed with GDPR and applicable EU AI Act requirements in mind

    We practice strict data minimisation. We do not collect unnecessary Personal Identifiable Information (PII), and chat logs are automatically purged after 30 days. Our bots feature mandatory, transparent AI disclosures so customers always know who they are speaking with.

    Your data, and your customers' data, are never used to train third-party AI models.
